LONDON, Oct. 21. -- Tom Douglas, who achieved success here in "Merton of the Movies" and "Fata Morgana," is now playing the principal role in another American importation called "Growing Pains," which is a new title for Booth Tarkington's "Seventeen." As with "Merton of the Movies," Douglas received more praise from the critics than did the play itself.
