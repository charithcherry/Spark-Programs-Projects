MADISON, Wis., March 30 -- One of the popular movies in town is "The War at Home," a documentary on how the movement against the Vietnam War engulfed the University of Wisconsin campus here in the 60's and early 70's. Since late last year, students have been flocking to see it and to cheer the radicalism of their predecessors.
