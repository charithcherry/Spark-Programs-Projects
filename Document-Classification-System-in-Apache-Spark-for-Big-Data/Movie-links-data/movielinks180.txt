THESE are the days of gardens gloriously abloom. With a movie camera, color film, care, thought and a few inexpensive accessories, a garden in all its loveliness, with all its little inhabitants -- grasshoppers, caterpillars, bees, beetles, butterflies -- can be preserved forever, the only loss being the fragrance.
