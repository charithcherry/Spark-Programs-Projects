THE musical program of the Mark Strand remains the same as last week, as D.W. Griffith's "Sally of the Sawdust" has been held over. The prelude is played by the symphony orchestra with Carl Edouarde conducting. Special music and settings have been provided for "The Circus Band" and Joseph Plunkett's circus prologue to the feature photoplay.
