PROPAGANDA pictures having proved to be a costly mistake, the studios now are using the war simply as a background for lusty, full-throated melodrama. Not one of the several films devoted to arousing America against Germany has been more than a casual success at the box office and as a result pictures which deal with world conditions now are of the old fashioned cops-and-robbers stripe.
