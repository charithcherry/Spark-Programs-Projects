Show business has a long tradition of feeding upon itself. Entertainments and the lives of entertainers are constantly being processed into new products. The history of movies is stuffed with examples: Larry Parks as Al Jolson, Susan Hayward as  Jane Froman, Doris Day as Ruth Etting, Frank Sinatra as Joe E. Lewis or, more recently, Sissy Spacek as Loretta Lynn.
